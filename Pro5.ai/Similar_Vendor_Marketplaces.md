# IT Staffing Vendor Marketplaces Similar to Pro5.ai

**Purpose:** Platforms where ExonPro Innovations can register as a vendor/supplier partner to access enterprise client projects requiring Salesforce, Cloud, AI, Full-Stack, Data & Analytics talent.

**Date Researched:** 2026-05-11

---

## 1. Prosperix (formerly Crowdstaffing)

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Website** | https://prosperix.com |
| **Type** | Hiring Marketplace + Vendor Management System (VMS) |
| **Description** | AI-powered hiring marketplace that matches open jobs to an expanding network of thousands of talent suppliers. The only VMS with a built-in hiring marketplace. Vendors select jobs they want to work on and earn rewards for successful placements. Prosperix handles onboarding, payroll, and compliance. |
| **How to Register** | Self-register at https://prosperix.com/self-register-to-the-hiring-marketplace or apply at https://prosperix.com/apply-to-become-a-supplier |
| **Key Benefit** | Zero business development effort needed -- immediate access to new job opportunities daily. Vendors get paid within 15 days after invoice submission. |
| **Markets** | US, Global |

## 2. Beeline (+ MBO Partners)

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Website** | https://www.beeline.com |
| **Type** | Vendor Management System (VMS) / Extended Workforce Platform |
| **Description** | Leading cloud-based VMS for managing contingent workforce and services procurement. Beeline acquired MBO Partners, combining VMS capabilities with employer/agent-of-record services and a talent marketplace. Used by large enterprises across healthcare, life sciences, financial services, and manufacturing. |
| **How to Register** | Supplier registration happens through enterprise client programs on the Beeline platform. Contact via https://www.beeline.com to explore supplier onboarding. |
| **Key Benefit** | Access to large enterprise clients who use Beeline as their VMS. Automates budgets, approvals, candidate selection, onboarding/offboarding, time & expense, and invoicing. |
| **Markets** | US, Global |

## 3. SAP Fieldglass

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Website** | https://www.fieldglass.com |
| **Type** | Vendor Management System / External Talent Marketplace |
| **Description** | SAP's cloud-based, open VMS that helps organizations find, engage, and manage external workforce and services procurement. Widely used by Fortune 500 companies. Integrates with SAP Ariba for end-to-end procurement. |
| **How to Register** | Register via the SAP Supplier Portal at https://www.sap.com/about/agreements/sap-supplier-portal/fieldglass.html. Supplier onboarding is typically initiated by enterprise clients who invite vendors into their Fieldglass programs. |
| **Key Benefit** | Massive enterprise client base -- being on SAP Fieldglass opens doors to Fortune 500 IT staffing requirements. |
| **Markets** | Global |

## 4. nextSource

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Website** | https://nextsource.com |
| **Type** | Managed Services Provider (MSP) / Supplier Alliance Program |
| **Description** | Vendor-neutral, technology-agnostic MSP that manages contingent workforce programs for enterprises. Maintains a Supplier Partnerships Office (SPO) with an all-inclusive supplier registration portal. Known for equitable treatment of vendors and fair business practices. |
| **How to Register** | Apply through the Supplier Alliances page at https://nextsource.com/supplier-alliances/ or email supplierpartnerships@nextsource.com. They also have a Keystone Supplier Program recognizing outstanding partners. |
| **Key Benefit** | Vendor-neutral approach means fair access to requisitions. Recognized supplier awards program (Staffing Partner of the Year, Outstanding Newcomer). |
| **Markets** | US |

## 5. TechnologyMatch

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Website** | https://technologymatch.com |
| **Type** | IT Vendor Reverse Marketplace |
| **Description** | A "Reverse Marketplace" where enterprise clients post project requirements anonymously, and pre-vetted vendors receive curated matches. Vendors are surfaced from a pre-vetted catalog based on client criteria. Contact details remain hidden until clients choose to engage, eliminating unsolicited outreach. |
| **How to Register** | Register as a vendor through the TechnologyMatch platform at https://technologymatch.com. Vendors go through a screening/vetting process before being added to the catalog. |
| **Key Benefit** | Quality-focused matching -- only relevant vendors see relevant opportunities. No cold outreach needed; clients come to you. |
| **Markets** | US, Global |

## Recommended Priority for ExonPro Innovations

Based on alignment with ExonPro's Salesforce, Cloud, AI, and Data & Analytics focus:

| Priority | Platform | Reason |
|----------|----------|--------|

| **Medium** | **SAP Fieldglass** | Fortune 500 access, but requires client invitation typically. |

| **Lower** | **Field Nation** | More field service/infrastructure focused than enterprise consulting. |
