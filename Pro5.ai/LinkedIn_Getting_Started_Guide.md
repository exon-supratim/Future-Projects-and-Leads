## IMPORTANT DO's AND DON'Ts

### DO
- Complete your profile BEFORE reaching out
- Always add a note with connection requests
- Be patient - people may take a few days to accept
- Keep messages professional but friendly
- Engage with their content genuinely

### DON'T
- Don't send connection requests with an empty/incomplete profile
- Don't send the detailed message AS the connection request (too long, will get rejected)
- Don't send both requests on the same day (looks spammy)
- Don't follow up aggressively if they haven't accepted yet
- Don't copy-paste generic messages - the ones I've prepared are already personalized
- Don't send messages outside business hours (aim for Tue-Thu, 9am-11am their timezone)
